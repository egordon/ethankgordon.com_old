About Me

Currently, I work for Oculus Virtual Reality on core tracking and human understanding technologies with the goal of improving visual comfort. My undergraduate study at Princeton University focused on engineering physics and silicon photonics, and my undergraduate thesis covered the design and control of integrated photonic neural networks.
Now, I am shifting my focus towards human-computer and human-robot interaction. My professional goal is to expand human knowledge in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering not just for its own sake, but to understand our human faculties, increase accessibility, and ultimately improve our collective quality of life. My most recent personal projects have targeted the deaf and hard-of-hearing.

In my free time, I enjoy singing and playing piano. I am a proud alumnus of Acapellago and current member of The Vocal Network.
